About Me

I grew up in the Northern Virginia Area. I was lucky enough to have lots of family close by that I could spend time with. I've been married for 5 years and have two beautiful girls; 3 and 1 years old.

I love to go to the beach with my family in the summer. We make sand castles, go boogey boarding, and crash into the waves. About every two years, as a family, we go to Disney World in Orlando, FL. and we have the time of our lives.
